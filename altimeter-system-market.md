<p><strong>The Altimeter System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Barometric Leveling</li><li>Laser Altimetry</li><li>Radar Altimetry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The altimeter system market encompasses three primary types: Barometric Leveling, Laser Altimetry, and Radar Altimetry. Barometric Leveling measures altitude based on atmospheric pressure variations, commonly used in weather forecasting and aviation. Laser Altimetry utilizes laser pulses to gauge distances, providing precise elevation data suited for topographic surveys and environmental monitoring. Radar Altimetry employs radio waves to determine land and sea surface heights, effectively used in satellite missions for earth observation, mapping, and climate research.</p></p>
