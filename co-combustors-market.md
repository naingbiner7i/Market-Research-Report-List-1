<p><p>The CO combustors market is categorized into three main types: Can, Cannular, and Annular combustors. Can combustors consist of individual combustion chambers for efficient heat management and are commonly used in industrial applications. Cannular combustors feature multiple combustion tubes, promoting better airflow and efficiency, making them suitable for aerospace engines. Annular combustors have a continuous ring design that maximizes combustion efficiency and minimizes emissions, often used in larger turbine engines. Each type serves distinct operational needs and performance benchmarks.</p></p>
