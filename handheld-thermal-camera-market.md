<p><p>The handheld thermal camera market is primarily divided into two types: monocular and binocular devices. Monocular thermal cameras feature a single lens for capturing thermal images, making them lightweight and easy to use for quick, one-handed operation. In contrast, binocular thermal cameras incorporate two lenses, allowing for depth perception and a more immersive viewing experience. Both types are utilized in various applications like surveillance, search and rescue, and wildlife observation, catering to different user preferences and operational needs.</p></p>
