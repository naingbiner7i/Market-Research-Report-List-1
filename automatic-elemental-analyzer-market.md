<p><strong>The Automatic Elemental Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>GC Chromatography</li><li>Frontal Chromatography</li><li>Adsorption-Desorption</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Elemental Analyzer market encompasses various types, including Gas Chromatography (GC), Frontal Chromatography, and Adsorption-Desorption. GC utilizes gas flow to separate and analyze elements based on their vaporization properties. Frontal Chromatography involves continuously introducing a sample to a stationary phase, leading to a direct measurement of component concentrations. Adsorption-Desorption techniques focus on the adherence of elements to a solid phase followed by their release for analysis, enhancing the sensitivity and accuracy of elemental detection in various applications.</p></p>
