<p><p>Total Lab Automation (TLA) systems can be categorized into three market types: closed, semi-closed, and open. Closed systems are proprietary, offering fully integrated solutions with limited flexibility but high efficiency, often seen in large labs. Semi-closed systems provide some integration while allowing for limited third-party components, balancing customization with efficiency. Open systems prioritize flexibility and compatibility, enabling extensive integration with diverse equipment, appealing to labs seeking adaptability in workflows and technology. Each type addresses specific operational needs and lab environments.</p></p>
