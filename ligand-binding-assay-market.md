<p><p>The ligand binding assay market is segmented into radioactive and non-radioactive types. Radioactive assays utilize radiolabeled ligands for detecting binding interactions, providing high sensitivity and specificity; however, they require stringent safety measures and regulatory compliance. Non-radioactive assays, including fluorescence, chemiluminescence, and colorimetric methods, offer safer alternatives with easier handling and reduced waste. Non-radioactive methods are increasingly preferred for their versatility and compatibility with high-throughput screening, driving growth in various applications, including drug discovery and diagnostics.</p></p>
