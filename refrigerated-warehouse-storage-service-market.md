<p><p>Refrigerated warehouse storage services can be categorized into public and private markets. Public refrigerated warehouses are facilities that offer storage space to multiple clients, allowing businesses to store perishable goods without the need for large capital investment. They typically provide flexible terms and scalable solutions. In contrast, private refrigerated warehouses are owned and operated by a single company, tailored specifically to its needs. This option offers greater control, customization, and efficiency but involves higher operational costs. Both types are essential for maintaining product integrity in the supply chain.</p></p>
