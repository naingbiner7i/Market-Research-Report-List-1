<p><p>Insect repellent towelettes are categorized into two main types: DEET and DEET-free. DEET towelettes contain the active ingredient DEET, known for its effectiveness in repelling a wide range of insects, including mosquitoes and ticks. They tend to offer long-lasting protection but may cause skin sensitivity in some users. DEET-free towelettes, on the other hand, utilize natural ingredients or alternative repellents to deter insects. These options appeal to consumers seeking safer, eco-friendly alternatives while providing varying degrees of efficacy.</p></p>
