<p><ul><li>Solvent Grade</li><li>Isomer Grade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mixed xylene market is categorized into two primary types: solvent grade and isomer grade. Solvent grade mixed xylene is primarily used as an industrial solvent in applications like paint thinners, adhesives, and coatings due to its effective solvency properties. In contrast, isomer grade mixed xylene is utilized mainly in chemical synthesis and as a feedstock in the production of chemicals like paraxylene, which is essential for producing polyester and other polymers. Each grade serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
