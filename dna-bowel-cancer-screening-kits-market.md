<p><p>DNA bowel cancer screening kits encompass two primary market types: blood sample and fecal sample tests. Blood sample tests analyze circulating DNA from tumors, offering a non-invasive method to detect cancer markers in the bloodstream. In contrast, fecal sample tests examine DNA from cells shed in stool, identifying genetic mutations associated with bowel cancer. Both methods aim to enhance early detection, improve patient outcomes, and increase screening accessibility, catering to diverse patient preferences and medical guidelines.</p></p>
