<p><p>The column-mounted industrial manipulator market includes three primary types: pneumatic, electric, and hydraulic manipulators. Pneumatic manipulators use compressed air for operation, offering lightweight handling and flexibility for smaller tasks. Electric manipulators rely on electric motors for precise control and energy efficiency, making them suitable for various applications requiring accuracy. Hydraulic manipulators utilize fluid pressure, providing high force and torque for heavy lifting tasks in demanding industrial environments. Each type serves distinct operational needs based on power source and application requirements.</p></p>
