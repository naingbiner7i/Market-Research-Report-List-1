<p><strong>The Double Deck Elevator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traction</li><li>Hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The double-deck elevator market can be categorized into two main types: traction and hydraulic. Traction double-deck elevators use ropes and pulleys for vertical movement, offering efficient energy use and higher speeds, making them ideal for skyscrapers and high-rise buildings. Hydraulic double-deck elevators, on the other hand, rely on fluid-driven pistons, providing smoother rides at lower heights, often preferred for mid-rise applications. Each type serves distinct architectural needs, balancing efficiency, speed, and capacity in urban environments.</p></p>
