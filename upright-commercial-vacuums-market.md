<p><strong>The Upright Commercial Vacuums Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-motor</li><li>Dual-motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Upright commercial vacuums are categorized into single-motor and dual-motor types. Single-motor vacuums use one motor to power both the brush and suction, offering a lightweight and cost-effective solution, ideal for small to medium spaces. In contrast, dual-motor vacuums feature separate motors for brushing and suction, delivering enhanced performance and deeper cleaning. This makes dual-motor vacuums better suited for larger areas or heavy debris, often favored in commercial settings requiring more robust cleaning capabilities.</p></p>
