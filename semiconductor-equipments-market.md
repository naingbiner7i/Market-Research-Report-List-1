<p><p>The semiconductor equipment market encompasses various technologies essential for chip manufacturing. Etching equipment removes material to create patterns on substrates. Thin film deposition equipment applies layers of materials to form circuits. Lithography equipment projects intricate designs onto surfaces to define chip features. Semiconductor process control equipment ensures precise monitoring and adjustment of manufacturing parameters. Additionally, compound semiconductor equipment focuses on materials like gallium nitride or indium phosphide, crucial for advanced applications in optoelectronics and high-frequency devices.</p></p>
