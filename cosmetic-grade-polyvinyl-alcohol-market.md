<p><p>Cosmetic Grade Polyvinyl Alcohol (PVA) is available in two main types: fully hydrolyzed and partially hydrolyzed. Fully hydrolyzed PVA offers superior solubility and film-forming properties, making it ideal for products like facial masks and hair care items. Partially hydrolyzed PVA, on the other hand, retains some vinyl acetate, providing flexibility and improved adhesion. This type is often used in formulations requiring durability and water resistance, such as cosmetics with longer-lasting applications. Both variants cater to specific formulation needs in the cosmetic industry.</p></p>
