<p><p>The prefabricated home market can be categorized into two main types: permanent and relocatable homes. Permanent prefabricated homes are designed for long-term use, often resembling traditional houses, and are placed on a fixed foundation. In contrast, relocatable homes, also known as modular or mobile homes, can be easily transported and relocated as needed. This flexibility makes relocatable homes ideal for temporary living solutions or for those seeking mobility, while permanent homes focus on durability and stability for extended occupancy.</p></p>
