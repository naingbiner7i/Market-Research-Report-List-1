<p><strong>The High Pressure Booster Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Multiple Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High pressure booster pumps are categorized into single stage and multiple stage pumps based on their design and application. Single stage pumps consist of one impeller, ideal for applications requiring lower pressure boosts and simpler installation. In contrast, multiple stage pumps feature several impellers arranged in series, allowing for higher pressure output, making them suitable for demanding applications such as water supply in high-rise buildings and industrial processes. Both types are essential for enhancing fluid flow and overcoming pressure challenges.</p></p>
