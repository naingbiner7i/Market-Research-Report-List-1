<p><ul><li>RS</li><li>GPS</li><li>CPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Silicon Nitride Ceramics market is categorized into three main types: Reaction-Sintered (RS), Gas Pressure Sintered (GPS), and Cold-Pressed Sintered (CPS) ceramics. RS ceramics are produced through a chemical reaction, offering good mechanical properties and thermal stability. GPS ceramics utilize gas pressure during sintering, resulting in higher density and strength. CPS ceramics are formed by cold pressing and sintered, providing a cost-effective option with moderate performance. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance requirements and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
