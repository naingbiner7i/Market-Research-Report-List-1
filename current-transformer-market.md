<p><p>The current transformer market features multiple types, including dry type, pouring type, oil-immersed, and gas-insulated transformers. Dry type transformers are compact and require minimal maintenance, making them suitable for indoor applications. Pouring type transformers utilize resin for insulation, offering enhanced safety in harsh environments. Oil-immersed transformers provide effective cooling and are widely used in substations. Gas-insulated transformers use high-voltage insulation gas, enabling compact designs and reliability in limited spaces, making them ideal for urban settings and critical applications.</p></p>
