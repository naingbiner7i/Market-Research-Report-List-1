<p><ul><li>Mechanical Seals</li><li>O Ring Seals</li><li>Lip Seals</li><li>Rotary Seals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile seals market encompasses various types, including mechanical seals, O-ring seals, lip seals, and rotary seals. Mechanical seals are designed to prevent fluid leakage in applications like pumps. O-ring seals provide effective sealing in static and dynamic environments, often used in powertrains. Lip seals effectively retain lubricants while blocking contaminants, typically found in axle and crankshaft applications. Rotary seals are engineered for rotating shafts, ensuring durability and leakage prevention in systems such as engines and transmissions. Together, they enhance vehicle performance and reliability.</p></p>
