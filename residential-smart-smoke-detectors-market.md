<p><ul><li>Photoelectric</li><li>Dual Sensors</li><li>Ionization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential smart smoke detector market features three primary types: photoelectric, dual sensor, and ionization detectors. Photoelectric detectors use a light source to sense smoke, providing quicker alerts for smoldering fires. Ionization detectors employ radioactive material to detect fast-flaming fires, offering rapid response to open flames. Dual sensor models combine both technologies, enhancing detection capabilities for various fire types. These innovative devices often integrate smart features, allowing remote monitoring and alerts via smartphones, improving home safety and response times.</p></p>
