<p><p>The air-cooled scroll chiller market can be categorized into two primary types: multiple compressor and single compressor systems. Multiple compressor chillers utilize several compressors to enhance efficiency, offer redundancy, and allow for variable capacity control, making them suitable for larger applications or fluctuating loads. In contrast, single compressor chillers are simpler, often more compact, and cost-effective, ideal for smaller applications with stable cooling needs. Both types leverage scroll technology for effective heat exchange and energy efficiency.</p></p>
