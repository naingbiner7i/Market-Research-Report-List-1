<p><p>The sustainable barrier paper market is divided into two main types: single-sided coating and double-sided coating. Single-sided coating involves applying a barrier layer to one side of the paper, offering protection from moisture and grease while allowing for printing or branding on the uncoated side. In contrast, double-sided coating provides barrier properties on both sides, enhancing durability and making it ideal for applications requiring greater resistance to external elements. Both types aim to reduce environmental impact while maintaining functionality.</p></p>
