<p><p>Next-generation payment technologies enhance transaction security and convenience. EMV chip technology, embedded in cards, safeguards against counterfeiting. Near Field Communication (NFC) enables quick, touchless payments via smartphones or contactless cards. Magnetic Secure Transmission (MST) broadcasts payment data using magnetic signals, allowing compatibility with traditional card readers. Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) facilitates low-power communication for secure transactions over short distances. Quick Response Codes (QR Codes) enable payments by scanning codes with mobile devices, streamlining transactions for customers and businesses alike.</p></p>
