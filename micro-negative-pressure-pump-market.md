<p><p>The Micro Negative Pressure Pump market comprises several types, each serving distinct applications. Diaphragm type pumps utilize a flexible membrane to create a vacuum, making them ideal for precise fluid handling. Electromagnetic type pumps employ electromagnetic forces to drive fluid motion, known for their efficiency and compactness. Impeller type pumps use rotating blades to generate negative pressure, suitable for higher flow rates. Other types may include rotary or peristaltic pumps, each with unique features for specialized uses in industries like healthcare and electronics.</p></p>
