<p><p>The plastic core market consists of two primary types: monolayer and double-layer. Monolayer cores are made from a single layer of plastic, offering cost-effectiveness and simplicity, making them suitable for various applications where strength and rigidity are essential. In contrast, double-layer cores feature two distinct layers, allowing for enhanced properties such as improved impact resistance, thermal stability, and moisture barrier capabilities. This dual-layer design is ideal for specialized applications, like packaging and construction, where performance and durability are critical.</p></p>
