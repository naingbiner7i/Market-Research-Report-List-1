<p><strong>The PET Strapping Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embossed PET Strapping</li><li>Smooth PET Strapping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PET strapping market is primarily divided into two types: embossed PET strapping and smooth PET strapping. Embossed PET strapping features a textured surface, providing enhanced grip and reducing slippage during transit. This type is often used in heavy-duty applications to secure products tightly. In contrast, smooth PET strapping has a sleek surface, allowing for easier feeding through strapping machines and less wear on equipment. Both types are used in various industries, including packaging, logistics, and construction, offering durability and reliability for securing goods.</p></p>
