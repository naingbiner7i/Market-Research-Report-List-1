<p><strong>The Rayon Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Viscose Staple Fiber</li><li>Viscose Filament Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rayon market is primarily categorized into two types: viscose staple fiber and viscose filament fiber. Viscose staple fiber is short, used mainly in textiles for clothing and home furnishings, offering softness and absorbency. In contrast, viscose filament fiber consists of long continuous strands, suitable for high-end fabrics and applications such as silk-like materials. Both segments cater to diverse consumer needs, with the staple fiber focusing on affordability and versatility, while the filament fiber emphasizes luxury and performance in textiles.</p></p>
