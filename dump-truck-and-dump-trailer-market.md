<p><p>The dump truck and trailer market includes three primary types: end dump, bottom dump, and side dump. End dump trucks unload material by tilting the trailer, making them ideal for loose aggregates. Bottom dump trailers discharge through a gate at the bottom, allowing for controlled placement of materials, suitable for road construction. Side dump trailers unload materials laterally, providing efficient side delivery of bulk materials in tight spaces. Each type serves specific applications based on material handling needs.</p></p>
